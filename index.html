Join the village and make real connections.

REAL Connections offers a village-style approach to aging in place at home. We connect seniors with fellow seniors and community members willing to lend a helping hand. That might mean getting or giving a ride to the doctor, finding a trusted handyman, or having someone to call.

Whatever it is, members in our village build new friendships and find peace of mind knowing they have a community to turn to.
