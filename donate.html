For the Supported Membership Fund

The Supported Membership Fund provides financial assistance and scholarships for community members wishing to join the Real Connections village.

$50 pays for a month of individual membership.

$75 pays for a month of household membership.

$570 pays for a year of individual membership.

$780 pays for a year of household membership.
