Vetted Service Provider Network

Complementing our “volunteer first” approach, vetted service providers add depth and expertise to REAL Connections and provide members with services that our research shows are priority needs and interests.

Referrals to a vetted network of service providers will enable members to access with confidence a broad array of high-demand services at pre-negotiated or discounted rates.

Prospective service providers are subject to pre-screening and registration, and agree to a set of customer service standards. Members of our provider network will be expected to offer priority appointments to members as feasible, and to provide preferred pricing wherever possible.
